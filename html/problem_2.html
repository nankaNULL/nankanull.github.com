Description

Loy要考验吉祥物cjc的智商，她让吉祥物判断一个数和2,3的关系。

Input

输入一个自然数n。

Output

如果n能被3和2整除，则输出”n divisible by 2 and 3? YES”，否则输出”n divisible by 2 and 3? NO”；

如果n能被3整除或者能被2整除，则输出”n divisible by 2 or 3? YES”，否则输出”n divisible by 2 or 3? NO”；

如果n能被2,3其中一个整除则输出”n divisible by 2 or 3, but not both? YES”，否则输出”n divisible by 2 or 3, but not both? NO”。

Samples

input:
18
output:
18 divisible by 2 and 3? YES
18 divisible by 2 or 3? YES
18 divisible by 2 or 3, but not both? NO
